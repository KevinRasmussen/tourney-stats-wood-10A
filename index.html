📋 Production Operational Procedures

1. Persistence Rules

  • Data is synced to the cloud. **Rosters and Opponents persist** through browser refreshes or device changes.
  • **Switch Safety:** Changing the active pitcher while a count is active will prompt a warning to prevent accidental loss of uncommitted pitches.

2. Opponent Management

  • Opponents are grouped by **Team**.
  • The "Game Total" for opponents is calculated by looking for stints logged against "Our Team".
