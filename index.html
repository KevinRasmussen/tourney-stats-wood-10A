📋 How to Use This Tracker (Click to Expand / Collapse)

1. Live Setup & Rules

  • Set **GAME MAX** and **TOURNEY MAX** thresholds at the top left. Color alerts switch automatically based on these numbers.
  • Type the current **Opponent Team** name and pick the **Inning** before starting a defensive tracking stretch.

2. Counting & Committing

  • Select a pitcher from the dropdown menu to sync live data tracking.
  • Use **+1 PITCH** for active counts. **Shortcut:** You can also tap directly on the giant numbers in the center to type a flat count manually.
  • Click **COMMIT STINT TO LOG** to clear the big screen and lock counts permanently into database metrics.

3. Opponent Tracking & Team Groupings

  • Switch to the **Opponent Pitchers** tab and click **+ Add Opponent Pitcher**.
  • Enter their **Jersey #** and **Team Name**. The app automatically groups all tracked pitchers together by team.
  • Type the *total number of previous tournament games* they pitched in. The app will loop and prompt you for **Game 1 Pitches**, **Game 2 Pitches**, etc., automatically summing up their tournament total.

4. Fixing Dugout Mistakes

  • **Wrong count logged?** Click a player's line in the log to expand it, and click 🗑️ next to the bad line item.
  • **Need quick adjustments?** Put a negative number (like `-5`) in the center box and tap **Commit** to deduct counts immediately.
